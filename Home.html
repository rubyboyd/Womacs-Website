Welcome to the WOMACS Website, Oberlin's group for Women in Math and Computer Science! Our mission is to provide support and encouragement to women interested in computer science and math. Whether you are a major, a complete newbie, or anything in between, you are welcome to attend meetings and events and be involved in any way you feel comfortable. Also, take a look at our Research and Resources page where you will find lots of information on the resources available to you, and where we have provided a lot of research which we hope you find interesting!
